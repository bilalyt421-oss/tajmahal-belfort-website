About Us

Welcome to Tajmahal Belfort

Experience the authentic flavors of India and Pakistan at Restaurant Tajmahal Belfort. Our culinary journey brings you traditional recipes passed down through generations, prepared with the finest ingredients and spices.

Whether you're craving aromatic curries, tender tandoori specialties, or fragrant biryani, our experienced chefs craft each dish with passion and expertise. We invite you to savor the rich cultural heritage of South Asian cuisine in an elegant and welcoming atmosphere.

Our Mission

To bring the authentic taste of India and Pakistan to Belfort, while providing exceptional service and creating unforgettable dining experiences for our guests.

Our Menu

Tandoori Specialties

Succulent meats and vegetables marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in our traditional clay oven. Tender, flavorful, and aromatic.

Curries & Gravies

Rich and creamy curries prepared with fresh herbs, tomatoes, and coconut milk. From mild to spicy, there's something for every palate.

Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with tender meat or vegetables, layered with aromatic spices. A complete meal in one dish.

Bread & Rice

Freshly baked naan, paratha, and roti. Perfectly cooked basmati rice and aromatic pilafs to complement your meal.

Appetizers

Samosas, pakoras, kebabs, and more. Start your meal with our delicious selection of traditional appetizers.

Desserts

Sweet treats including gulab jamun, kheer, and traditional Indian sweets. The perfect ending to your dining experience.
